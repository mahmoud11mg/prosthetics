Prostheses are prosthetic medical devices used to replace the loss of limbs, whether the loss is due to accident, disease or obstructed birth.

Although prostheses cannot provide the same full natural functions as natural limbs, they are able to compensate for some basic functions such as walking and moving.

Yes I prostheses can be customized to fit an individual's unique needs, usually by individually designing and manufacturing prostheses to an individual's specifications and standards.

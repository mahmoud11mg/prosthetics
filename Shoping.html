AvalonK2

AvalonK2

AvalonK2

The AvalonK2 Foot  is the latest hydraulic foot/ankle system from Endolite. It enhances walking confidence  because it hydraulically adjusts to inclines and steps, the AvalonK2 self-aligns to secure the knee joint and encourage good posture and joint position, this enhances trans-femoral knee stability to help prevent falls and it reduces unwanted moments on the knee joint of trans-tibial users. The toes dorsi-flex after mid stance and remain elevated during swing phase providing increased ground clearance for safety and efficiency.
